Frequently Asked Questions

What type of work are you looking for?

We showcase work across all mediums including painting, sculpture, photography, and digital art. We prioritize work that demonstrates conceptual depth and technical skill.

Is there a submission fee?

No, submissions are free. Our gallery is committed to supporting emerging artists and providing accessible opportunities.

Who can sumbit?

While we prioritize students and emerging artists, we consider all applications. If you're in the early stages of your career or exploring new directions in your practice, we encourage you to apply.

What happens if my work is selected?

Selected artists work closely with our curatorial team to plan the exhibition, including installation, promotional materials, and opening reception. We provide support throughout the process and help with documentation.
