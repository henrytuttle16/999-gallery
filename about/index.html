About

99.9 Gallery is a student and emerging artist-run space dedicated to fostering creative expression and providing exhibition opportunities for new voices in contemporary art.

We believe in making art accessible to our community and creating a supportive environment where artists can experiment, grow, and connect with audiences.

What We Do

Exhibitions

We host 6-8 exhibitions per year, each running 4-6 weeks. Solo or group presentations curated by our team.

Open Calls

We accept submissions year-round. Students, recent grads, and self-taught artists welcome. No fees.

Community

Opening nights, art strolls, and the occasional gathering. We're building community, not just a calendar.
